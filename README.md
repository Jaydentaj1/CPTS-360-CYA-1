# CPTS-360-CYA-1
Career Development Experience: Choose Your Own Adventure 01

My chosen adventure was applying for a part time job at The Coug so that I could have an extra source of income while I finish college. It will also help me learn some essential life experiences that I can take with me for applying to my career and keeping with me in life.

My career goals involve lessons and skills that improve my weaknesses as a career professional and help with my career and life experiences. One of the most essential skills I can takeaway from a college job is my social skills because one of my biggest weaknesses is I have social anxiety.
This affects most of my social interactions when I am put in a new or uncomfortable experience and it hinders my ability to meet new people and cooperate with others. I think this is the most important skill I will benefit from with this job along with other common experiences that come with the job.

The whole experience and expectations of the application process were an application online and providing my weekly college schedule to give the hiring team my availability while I am in college. The application didn't require a resume, but instead required filling out an online application form that asked the necessary questions for the job experience.
